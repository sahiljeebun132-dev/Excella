Ready to Join?

We're looking for passionate gamers at every level. No prior esports experience required — just dedication and the will to compete for MDX.

  • Minimum 4 hours per week practice commitment
  • Positive team attitude and good communication
  • Attend weekly sessions & scheduled tournaments
  • Join our Discord and stay active in the community
